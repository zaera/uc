the material has this structure:

Fake_glass

 - Reflection
	- Fresnel 
		max230
		min60
 - diffuse
	color 
		200,204,225
	transparecy
		- blend, 
			A = noise, perlin, size 0.25
			B = A color, black
			ratio 0.25
 - NO REFRACTION
 - NO DOUBLE SIDE
 - Bump
	- Noise, size 0.1
 - Reflection
	- Bitmap, spherical HDR, esterno_giorno_01.hdr


You can play with blend amount in trasparency
and the noise size
